# PeopleMusic - Marketing Brief

**Date:** January 2026
**Status:** MVP Complete, Ready for Rollout

## What It Is

PeopleMusic is a public database that tracks whether music artists are human or AI-generated. Think "verified checkmark" but for proving you're a real person making real music.

**The tagline:** *Is This Artist Human or AI?*

---

## Why It Matters Now

- **AI music is flooding streaming platforms.** Spotify removed 75M+ "spammy" tracks last year. Deezer gets 50,000 AI-generated uploads daily.
- **Listeners can't tell the difference.** 97% of people can't distinguish AI from human music (Deezer study).
- **AI artists are charting.** Multiple AI-generated songs hit Billboard charts in 2025â€”including #1 Country.
- **Artists are getting ripped off.** Fake "ghost artists" and AI clones steal playlist spots and royalties from real musicians.

**Nobody is tracking this.** No central authority exists to verify artist authenticity. We're building it.
